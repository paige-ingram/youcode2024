Name: Fission SV Mitten

Description:
The Fission  SV Mitten combines the proven performance of Fortius™ 1.0 with a waterproof and breathable GORE-TEX insert for full weather protection. PrimaLoft® Silver and Gold Insulation gives maximum warmth and an additional Octa® Loft breathable insulation lining next to skin manages moisture and makes for an easy on/off. Too add to the ruggedness, a double layer of premium goat leather reinforcements enhance durability. An elasticated wrist strap provides a precision fit and the pull to open/pull to close gauntlet ensures severe weather is sealed out.

Short Description:
Insulated and GORE-TEX weatherproof mitten with the durability and versatility for skiing, snowboarding and winter sports in severe conditions. | SV: Severe Weather.
