Name: Micon 32 Backpack

Description:
Peaceful uptracks, incredible descents, the views - this is why you choose the backcountry. Comfortable and durable, and the Micon 32 is optimized for tours with a balanced carry, weather protection, and intuitive organization. A dedicated pocket keeps avalanche tools right at hand, the roll-top main compartment stores your day’s kit, helmet carries externally, and a lid pocket stashes goggles and small essentials. Built for alpine conditions, its high tenacity N210r HT nylon and N400r-AC² ripstop combine for focused durability and weather protection, critical seams are taped, and zippers are WaterTight™.
